Task 1: sort according to tempo

In the following pages, please rank the given songs according to its tempo (i.e. speed/pace of the song).


Rank the songs in descending order of tempo: the faster the song, the higher up it ranks.

On the left hand side of the page you will see a Spotify Playlist (1).
Please listen to the songs, and rank them using the drag and drop interface on your right (2).
We suggest you jump through bits of the song at different timestamps (eg. in 30 second intervals) to get an overview of the tempo over the entire song.
Click continue when you are done (3).
